Spark Something


At The Sims, we believe in the power of creativity. Spark Something is a celebration of creativity and the role our games and brand have played in sparking the creative journey of rule breakers everywhere for over 23 years. It’s the declaration of our ongoing commitment to creators. And the optimistic, inclusive, future they shape by showing up authentically and creating fearlessly. Spark Something is a love note to creative potential. A celebration of our ability to inspire creativity in others. And encouragement to seek out that creative spark everywhere we go.
